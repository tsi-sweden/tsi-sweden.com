We provide Complete solutions for delivering Oil and Gas training programmes that meet international standards for producing skilled technicians who can maintain and operate upstream and downstream systems, including drilling operations, pipelines and refinery operations. We also offer a unique programme for training in the Petroleum Retail Sector.
  • Competency-based eLearning materials mapped to Internationally recognised qualifications
  • Hands-on hardware for the classroom
  • Hands-on hardware for workshops
  • Practical skills workshops complete with all tooling and equipment
  • Training rigs based on actual oil and gas systems components
  • Mini Plants for simulated on-the-job training, with computer inserted faults for full system diagnostics and operator scenario training in emergency procedures and start up and safe shut down
  • Computer-based Operator Training Simulator Systems (OTS) for refinery operations
